About Me

Educator, researcher, and lifelong learner at the intersection of language and machines.

I am Md. Siam Ansary, an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), Dhaka, Bangladesh.

I hold a Master in Information Technology (MIT) from the Institute of Information Technology (IIT), University of Dhaka, and a BSc in CSE from AUST — where I graduated with Dean's List of Honor. For my undergraduate thesis and master's project, I worked on Automatic Text Summarization.

My current research spans natural language processing, opinion mining, automatic text summarization, stock market analysis, and emerging applications of machine learning in structural health monitoring and food quality assessment.
